# ChocolateSalesAnalysis

Chocolate Sales Analysis Report
The Chocolate Sales Analysis project focuses on analysing sales performance for a chocolate business to uncover insights related to revenue, customer behaviour, product performance, and regional trends.
This project aims to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights using data analysis and visualisation techniques, enabling better business decision-making.
________________________________________
Objectives
•	Analyse overall sales performance and growth trends 
•	Identify top-performing and underperforming products and categories 
•	Understand customer purchasing behaviour based on age and gender 
•	Evaluate store performance across different locations and types 
•	Identify top and bottom customers for targeted marketing strategies 
•	Discover geographic trends in sales across countries and cities
________________________________________
Scope of Analysis
•	Overall Sales Analysis
•	Sales Analysis by Category
•	Sales Analysis by Brand
•	Sales Analysis by Products
•	Sales Analysis by Stores Country
•	Sales Analysis by City
•	Sales Analysis by Stores Type
•	Sales Analysis by Stores Name
•	Sales Analysis by Customers Age
•	Sales Analysis by Customers Gender
•	Sales Analysis by Top 10 Customers
•	Sales Analysis by Bottom 10 Customers
________________________________________
Sales Analysis Key Insights:

•	Ferrero brand has highest revenue and highest profit
•	Dark chocolate 50% is a highest selling product
•	Canada has highest revenue and profit 
•	Toronto city has highest revenue and profit
